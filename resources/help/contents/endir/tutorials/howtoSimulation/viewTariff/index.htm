Tutorial - How to simulate a Portfolio

3. View your simulation

Once you have filled your portfolio, TariffEye computes the fee taken for the different values you entered. Theses values are shown near the Tariff distribution tree, and change when you change the investment data entries of your portfolio. To change these values, use one of the methods described in the previous section "2. Fill-in your investment portfolio".

3. a: Manage your preferences

In addition to amount of moneys invested, or volumes of transactions, which are defined by the Worksheets of the different positions, tarifications files contain several important customer-related informations to determine how much the investment service costs.
This information is located in a special preference position in the lowest level of the Tariff distribution tree. The content of this node is defined by the tarification file, but the kind of options is always one of the following:

  • A simple choice, true or false.
  • A multiple choice, with a set of possible answers.
  • A value, as a percentage or a money amount.

These values can be set-up during the simulation. To do so, follow these steps:

Step by step

  1. Select the preferences node into the Tariff distribution tree

  2. Modify the value of an option

The position where an option is used also shows the value of this option. You can also change it at this place, the changes will change in accordance at the preferences position.

6. Print simulation results

TariffEye gives you the ability to make efficient reports of your actual simulation, or even a complete report of the tarification that was used for this simulation.

Step by step process

In the Reports menu of the simulator interface, you canlaunch three types of reports.


Each of these three menus will open a new window (a viewer) that will allow you to review the report on screen and to print it to any printer that would be configured on your computer.


The first menu: "Fee report summary..." will lead to a resumed report about costs repartition over the simulated portfolio.
The second menu: "Fee detailed report..." will give some more information concerning the exact content of the portfolio and its associated costs.
The third menu: "Tarification report..." produces a document which is the paper version of the tarification used for the simulation.
